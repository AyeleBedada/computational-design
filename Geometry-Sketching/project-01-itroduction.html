1.5.1, Project on Development and construction of Transition pieces (Transition Polyhedron and solids of revolution)

After you are done working on the project, submit your work at this google form.

To work on this project, students need to be organized in to groups containing five or less number of students. You are expected to master the development and construction of Transition pieces (Transition polyhedron and solids of revolution). Furthermore you should explore the application of these geometries in the built environment and design. As such this project is designed to examine your understanding of transition pieces (Transition polyhedron and solids of revolution) and their application in design and architecture. You should incorporate the knowledge and skills you acquired during lectures, tutorials and studio exercise to complete this task. The project is a group work and has two components:

(1) Report containing (Maximum of six pages without including cover page, table of content and bibliography/ reference). Students must use both inline citation and references while preparing the report:
(a) a detailed reflection on the summary you can find at this link: Summary
• Category of solids based on the degree of regularity/ irregularity and method of development
• Methods of surface development
• Application, Principles, and Rules of surface development
• Materials and techniques in surface development
(b) Report on the specific project you are working on: Drawings, Text, and chart, pictures describing geometrical analysis, construction technique, and application of material, function, structure, aesthetics, and sustainability.

(2) The physical prototype/ 1 to 1 scale construction of the transition piece.

1.5.2, Development and Model of Irregular Polyhedron (An optional Task)

***This project is not intended for evaluation. It is intended to create an optional opportunity for students to develop their skill in developing and modeling irregular polyhedrons.

You are expected to master the development and construction of regular and irregular polyhedron and solids of revolution. Furthermore you should explore the application of these geometries in the built environment. As such this project is designed to examine your understanding of irregular polyhedron and its application in design and architecture. You should incorporate the knowledge and skills you acquired during lectures, tutorials and studio exercise to complete this task.

Selected building for surface development and model task: " Reflection of Mineral is a “gem” of architecture designed by Atelier Tekuto. It is a modern response to the use of minimal space. Sitting at the intersection of two streets, at 44m2 legal conditions and the client’s wish for a roofed garage pushed the architects to explore the limits of minimalist architecture." aRCH20

1.2, Parametric modeling without scripting (visual programming or hard coding)

On the former subunit, you have brain stormed and exercised on fundamentals of parameters, constraints and expressions. On this sub-unit you will further explore, demonstrate and create basic parametric design without embarking on scripting (Visual programming oe coding). On the coming unit of the course you will study and work with visual programming and hard coding. You will explore and work with different related software and plugins.

The above image depicts activities you will be embarking on under this subunit: Parametric modeling without scripting (visual programming or hard coding).

Software such as Revit and rhino provide the opportunity to work with parametric projects without visual programming and hard coding. This subunit uses these opportunity to allow you expand on your study on the basics of parametric design.

The experience you will gain on the software under consideration while studying and working on the demonstrations and exercises of this sub unit is applicable / transferable to your future architectural modeling unit

0.4: Pre-course assessment instruction

At the subunits of unit 0.3 and 0.4 You will find two forms "Pre-course Assessment-1" and "Pre-course Assessment-2" consecutively. Fill the first form on Friday, October 4, from 12:30 PM to 6:30 PM EAT. You should not refer to your notes, text books and the internet to provide feedback for the first form.

Fill the second form at home after you made a careful study and research on the themes of the questions under consideration. You should use your own words and expressions while providing feedback. Incase you need to borrow other authors' words and expressions, you should properly cite them with APA (American Psychological Association) Citation style. You can optionally read a note on APA: A short note on APA style citation. The second form will be available from Saturday, October 5, 2024 7:30 AM to Wednesday, October 09, 2024 12:30 PM EAT.

Make sure not to miss the given time intervals to provide your feedbacks.

You should cite AI services if you use any. However if english is not your first language, we recommend for you to compose your texts manually in order to strengthen your english language skill. The successful completion of both tasks will contribute to your total marks on the course with 5%.

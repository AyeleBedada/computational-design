1.4.2, Assignment on true length, shapes, angles, intersections, visibility, and Irregular Solids

As part of basic exercise for the course, you should work on the following two tasks:

(1) Computing true length, true shapes, true/dihedral Angle and visibility. You should print Page 1, 2, and 3 of the enclosed pages on separate A4 papers in a scale to fit for this task. Workout the required projections on the printouts and submit them to your instructor.

(2) Work on two dimensional developments and three dimensional (hard) modeling of irregular solids. Students should print page 4, 5, and 6 of the enclosed pages on separate A3 papers in scale to fit. You will work on the printouts to find the required true shapes of the faces of the indicated solids. Finally you will submit (a) The 2D workout/ projection drawings on the printed out A3 papers and (b) hard model of each solid separately.

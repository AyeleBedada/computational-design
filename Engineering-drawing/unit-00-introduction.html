'Unit-0' of the course, Engineering Drawing [COTM-2051], is dedicated to course Syllabus, Pre-Course Assessment, Grading and Rubrics. Navigate 'unit-0' by making use of the sidebar explore the contents and complete all tasks included according to instructions indicated.



Learning Objectives

Learning objective at course level


• Students will explore, decisive, demonstrate and apply geometry in design and construction
• Students will explore, decisive, demonstrate and apply drafting in design and construction

Learning objective at Unit level -Unit 1, Geometry


• Students will explore, describe and demonstrate geometry
• Students will explore, explain and demonstrate the application of geometry in design and construction.
• Students will analyze design and construction projects through three dimensional thinking.
• Students will form formal and spatial complex ideas through geometry addressing the complexity in architecture and construction.
• Students will create geometrically complex 3D objects and architectural components.

Learning objective at Unit level -Unit 2, Drafting (Architectural and Construction Drafting)


• Students will explore, explain and demonstrate documentations in the(Architecture, engineering and construction industry) AEC
• Students will explore, explain and demonstrate documentation coordination/ integration among different disciplines
• Students will create architectural and construction drawings

Learning Objectives

Course Learning Objectives>

Unit 1, Geometry
• Students will explore and describe geometry
• Students will explore, explain and demonstrate the application of geometry in design and construction.
• Students will analyze design and construction projects through three dimensional thinking.
• Students will form formal and spatial complex ideas through geometry addressing the complexity in architecture and construction.
• Students will create geometrically complex 3D objects and architectural components.
• Students will explore and explain the connection between geometry and digital modeling, in computational design.
Unit 2, Drafting • Students will explore, explain and demonstrate documentations in the(Architecture, engineering and construction industry) AEC
• Students will explore, explain and demonstrate documentation coordination/ integration among different disciplines
• Students will create architectural and construction drawings

1.2.4, Activity/ Assignment Regular Solids

As part of basic exercise for the course you should practice on fundamentals of geometric solids. You will work on three separate tasks over the course of the coming two weeks. The tasks are: Part one (one of the five platonic solids (regular convex polyhedron)); Part two (one of the four Kepler-Poinsot solids (Regular concave polyhedron)) and part three (one of the thirteen Archimedean solids (Semi-Regular Polyhedron)).

You should deliver two dimensional developments of the three polyhedron solids indicated above on appropriate paper size and with all necessary information (dimension, labeling, line type and quality, title, etc.) and Hard models of the three indicated polyhedron. Neatness, accuracy is important. Materials should be completed and submitted as per the indicated schedule on the assignment description below.

You can view the assignment description document about the assignment on regular polyhedron below. You can only view this file if you are signed in on your browser with the email you registered for this course. You may also see examples from past years as a reference on the image slide below.

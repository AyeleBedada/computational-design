1.2, Tessellation Assignment

As part of basic exercise for the course, you should practice on fundamentals of geometric shapes. You should incorporate your knowledge and skills you acquired during lectures and studio exercise to complete this task.

For this week, create a composition of your interest based on the types of tessellations you discussed in class during lecture.

You should carryout the task on an A4 paper. You can use ink, pencil or other material of your choice.

You can use the following examples done by students from the past years as a reference:

After you complete your work scan the image (jpg/png 300KB or less in size) and publish your work on one of the three padlets (the padlet you are assigned to) at the end of this page:

The first one is for students from number 1 to 20.
The second one is for students from number 21 to 40
The third one is for students from number 41 to 51.

(1) One scanned image of your work (300KB or less, this is very important).

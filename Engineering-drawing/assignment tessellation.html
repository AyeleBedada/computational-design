1.2, Tessellation Assignment

As part of basic exercise for the course, you should practice on fundamentals of geometric shapes. You should incorporate your knowledge and skills you acquired during lectures and studio exercise to complete this task.

For this week, create a composition of your interest (tiling for your living room, mural for your favorite surface, pattern for cover of your notebook or clothing, cladding for your favorite wall, etc) based on the types of tessellations you discussed in class during lecture.

You should carryout the task on an A4 paper. You can use ink, pencil or other material of your choice.

On this task, You will be evaluated by making use of the third rubrics type, 2.3, Single Point Rubrics.
